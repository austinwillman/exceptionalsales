What It Is

A Prospecting EKG —
Not a Personality Test

The SPQ*Gold (Sales Preference Questionnaire) measures 16 distinct types of Sales Call Reluctance — from role rejection and emotional inhibition to hyper-professionalism and referral aversion.

Unlike personality assessments that tell you who you are, SPQ*Gold tells you specifically where your prospecting behavior breaks down — and gives you a precise starting point for fixing it.
